About Us

*Veteran-Owned & Family Operated*

Welcome to The Den Jamaican Restaurant, a veteran-owned and family-operated gem where the vibrant flavors of Jamaica come alive. Founded by Tommy Isaacs, who transitioned from military service to sharing his passion for authentic Jamaican cuisine, our restaurant blends tradition and love in every dish. At The Den, we believe in the power of family and community, striving to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Join us and savor the rich, soulful flavors of Jamaica with every bite.
